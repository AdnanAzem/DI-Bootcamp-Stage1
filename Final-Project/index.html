Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward

Biography

Cristiano Ronaldo (born February 5, 1985) is a Portuguese footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest in history. Known for his athleticism and goal-scoring, he has won five Ballon d'Ors and set numerous records. Starting at Sporting CP, Ronaldo rose to fame with Manchester United, achieving Champions League and Premier League titles. He became Real Madrid's all-time top scorer, winning four Champions Leagues, before joining Juventus and later Al-Nassr. With Portugal, he won Euro 2016 and the Nations League.
